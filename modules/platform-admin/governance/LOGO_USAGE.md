## 1) Purpose

Define approved logo variants and usage rules for the Bassan platform. The logo is a brand asset owned by Shavi Company and must be used consistently.

**This document does NOT contain**:

- Image embeds or SVG code
- Specific pixel dimensions

**Evidence**: OWNERSHIP_AND_RIGHTS.md

---

## 2) Logo Philosophy

### 2.1 Logo Presence

**Logo is always present**:

- Small
- Calm
- Never dominant

**Logo exists to reassure, not to advertise**.

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 4

---

### 2.2 Logo Intent

**The logo provides**:

- Brand identity
- Reassurance
- Orientation

**The logo does NOT**:

- Dominate the interface
- Distract from work
- Advertise aggressively

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 4

---

## 3) Approved Logo Variants

### 3.1 Available Variants

Based on available assets, the following logo variants exist:

**Black Logo (BK)**:

- File: `bassan-BK.jpg`
- Use: Light backgrounds

**White Logo (WH)**:

- File: `bassan-WH.png`
- Use: Dark backgrounds

**Blue Logo (BU)**:

- File: `bassan-Bu.jpg`
- Use: Neutral or brand-colored backgrounds

**SVG Variants**:

- `BASSAN LOGO SVG bK BU.png` (Black + Blue)
- `BASSAN LOGO SVG WH BU.png` (White + Blue)

---

### 3.2 Variant Selection

**MUST select variant based on background**:

- Light background → Black or Blue logo
- Dark background → White or Blue logo
- Brand-colored background → Contrasting variant

**Rationale**: Logo must be clearly visible without harsh contrast.

---

## 4) Background Usage Rules

### 4.1 Light Backgrounds

**MUST use**:

- Black logo (BK) OR
- Blue logo (BU)

**MUST ensure**:

- Adequate contrast for visibility
- Logo does not blend into background

---

### 4.2 Dark Backgrounds

**MUST use**:

- White logo (WH) OR
- Blue logo (BU)

**MUST ensure**:

- Adequate contrast for visibility
- Logo does not blend into background

---

### 4.3 Colored Backgrounds

**MUST**:

- Select variant with sufficient contrast
- Test visibility on actual background color
- Prefer neutral variants (black/white) over colored

**MUST NOT**:

- Use logo on busy or patterned backgrounds
- Use logo on backgrounds with insufficient contrast

---

## 5) Clear Space Rules

### 5.1 Minimum Clear Space

**MUST maintain clear space around logo**:

- No text, graphics, or UI elements within clear space
- Clear space ensures logo visibility and impact

**Conceptual clear space**:

- Minimum: Height of logo itself
- Recommended: 1.5x height of logo

**Rationale**: Clear space prevents visual clutter and maintains logo integrity.

---

### 5.2 Clear Space Exceptions

**MAY reduce clear space in**:

- Extremely space-constrained contexts (mobile headers)
- Dense operational interfaces (admin views)

**MUST NOT**:

- Eliminate clear space entirely
- Allow logo to touch other elements

---

## 6) Logo Sizing

### 6.1 Size Philosophy

**Logo should be**:

- Small
- Calm
- Unobtrusive

**Logo should NOT be**:

- Large or dominant
- Attention-seeking
- Distracting

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 4

---

### 6.2 Minimum Size

**MUST maintain minimum size for**:

- Legibility
- Brand recognition
- Professional appearance

**Conceptual minimum**:

- Large enough to be recognized
- Small enough to remain calm

---

### 6.3 Maximum Size

**MUST NOT**:

- Use oversized logo
- Make logo the dominant visual element
- Use logo as hero image or splash screen (except startup animation)

**Exception**: Startup screen uses animated Bassan logo for warm-up and orientation.

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 9

---

## 7) Forbidden Usage

### 7.1 Distortion

**MUST NOT**:

- Stretch or compress logo (maintain aspect ratio)
- Rotate logo (except 90° increments if necessary)
- Skew or warp logo

---

### 7.2 Recoloring

**MUST NOT**:

- Change logo colors (use approved variants only)
- Apply gradients or effects to logo
- Reduce logo opacity (use at 100% opacity)

**Exception**: Subtle opacity reduction for disabled states (if necessary).

---

### 7.3 Modification

**MUST NOT**:

- Add elements to logo
- Remove elements from logo
- Combine logo with other graphics
- Use logo as part of a sentence or phrase

---

### 7.4 Background Violations

**MUST NOT**:

- Use logo on busy or patterned backgrounds
- Use logo on low-contrast backgrounds
- Use logo on backgrounds that obscure visibility

---

## 8) Logo Placement

### 8.1 Standard Placement

**Typical logo placement**:

- Top-left corner (navigation area)
- Small, calm presence
- Always visible (not hidden in collapsed menus)

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 4

---

### 8.2 Startup Screen

**Startup screen MUST**:

- Use animated Bassan logo
- Serve as warm-up, data hydration, orientation

**Evidence**: Brand & UI Constitution Section 9

---

### 8.3 Context-Adaptive Placement

**Logo placement MAY vary by**:

- Screen size (mobile vs. desktop)
- Context (admin vs. client)
- Layout constraints

**MUST maintain**:

- Logo visibility
- Calm presence
- Brand consistency
